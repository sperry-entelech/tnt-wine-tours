## USAGE NOTES

- Test one variable at a time for clean A/B results
- Run each test for minimum 2 weeks or 500 visitors
- The "Designated Driver" angle consistently resonates with group bookings
- "Free Guide" CTAs typically outperform "Book Now" for cold traffic
- "Book Now" CTAs perform better for warm/returning visitors
- Seasonal copy (spring/fall) can be swapped in during peak wine tour seasons
